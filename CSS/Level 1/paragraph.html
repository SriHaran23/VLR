WHAT IS A PARAGRAPH?

WHAT IS A PARAGRAPH? A paragraph is a block of sentences within a larger piece of writing, this piece of writing could be fictional, an essay, an article or any other piece of written text. Most commonly, a paragraph will be used to discuss a particular theme within the writing, for example, if a piece of writing is talking about animals, the author might use different paragraphs in order to discuss each individual animal to be talked about.
WHAT IS A PARAGRAPH USED FOR?
As we have discovered, the paragraph is used within a larger body of writing to separate ideas and themes, but there are many ways in which the paragraph functions.
